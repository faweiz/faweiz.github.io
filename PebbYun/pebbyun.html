Help

  • Long press on 'select' to activate voice recognition. (Pebble Time, Time Steel and Time Round only)
  • The menu names are used on your Pebble screen. They are also translated to the IFTTT Maker 'event name'. They are made lowercase and spaces become underscores. For example, menu 'Living room' becomes event 'living_room'.
  • If 'toggle' is ticked, then the menu item will have a checkbox on your Pebble screen. Turning off will send an '_off' suffix, turning on will send an '_on' suffix. For example, 'living_room_off' and 'living_room_on' respectively.
  • After finish naming your menus name, click 'save'
